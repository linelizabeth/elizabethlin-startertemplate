About

Hey there! I'm Elizabeth Lin, an interactive design student studying at Capilano University's IDEA School of Design.

I love to problem-solve armed with my toolkit of curiosity and thirst for knowledge. I am not afraid to be meticulous and dive deep into the core of my work while also resurfacing for the bigger picture. But no matter how far I may stray for inspiration, I will always come back to my principles—where purpose and function are crucial, and being analytical counts.

Want to work together or chat? Come say hey at heyo@elizabethlin.ca!
